# Cling! - Money Management App

"Manage Your Money, Reach Your Goals, and Thrive."

<img src="assets/cover.jpg" alt="Cover Cling!" style="width:700px;">

## About

Cling! is money tracking app that helps users manage their finances and keep track of their expenses. It provides a range of features and tools to help individuals and businesses stay on top of their financial health. 

Creating and sticking to a budget can be a daunting task. Many individuals struggle to establish realistic budgets and find it challenging to track their expenses and adjust their spending habits accordingly.
